[Algo Used]
1. (number>>1)<<1 = number, right shift then left shift is equal to number in case of even number 

Example :
a. 4>>1 = 0100>>1 = 0010 = 2 = (0010)<<1 = 0100 = 4 , even no 
b. 5>>1 = 0101>>1 = 0010 = 2 = (0010)<<1 = 0100 = 4 , odd no
